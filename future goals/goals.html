Ways to Differentiate My Project

1. AI-Powered Customization

  • Use AI to suggest invitation templates based on user preferences (e.g., wedding, birthday, or corporate event).
  • Auto-generate invitation text based on event details.

2. Interactive & Animated Invitations

  • Provide animated invitation templates that guests can interact with (e.g., RSVP buttons, countdown timers).
  • Offer GIF or short video invitation options.

3. One-Click Theme Selection

  • Let users pick a theme (e.g., floral, minimalist, vintage) and auto-adjust fonts, colors, and layouts.

4. Guest Management & RSVP Tracking

  • Enable users to send invites directly via email, WhatsApp, or SMS.
  • Provide real-time RSVP tracking and guest list management.

5. Augmented Reality (AR) Invitations

  • Add QR codes to invitations that display 3D animations or messages when scanned.

6. Eco-Friendly & Digital-First Approach

  • Promote sustainability by offering fully digital, eco-friendly invitations.
  • Provide an option for digital signatures or voice messages in invites.

7. Realistic Print Styles for Digital Cards

  • Mimic luxury wedding card styles with embossed effects, gold foiling, and premium textures.
